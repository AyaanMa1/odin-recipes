Fresh Salad

Description

A simple and refreshing salad with fresh vegetables and a light dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Toss them together in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Serve fresh.

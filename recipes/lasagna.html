Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles.
  2. Cook meat in sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, cheese.
  4. Bake 375°F (190°C) 45 minutes.

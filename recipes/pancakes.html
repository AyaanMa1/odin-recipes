Pancakes

Description

Light and fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast, served with syrup or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk egg and milk, then add to dry ingredients.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Cook on a hot pan until golden on both sides.
